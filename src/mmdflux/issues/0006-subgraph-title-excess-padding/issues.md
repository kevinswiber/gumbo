# Issues: Subgraph Title Excess Padding

## Date: 2026-01-29

Titled subgraphs have excess blank space above the first content node (3 blank lines between the title border and the first node). The bottom border also lacks a minimum padding of 1 line, causing it to sit flush against the last node in some cases.

**Source:** Direct observation after plan 0031 (post-rank title insertion)
